Goy Mukhya Mantri Prashikshanarthi Yojana (GMMPY) – Internship Scheme



Purpose:

To allow short term exposure of ‘selected candidates’ with the different Verticals/Divisions/Units of Project Management Unit (PMU)-CARES as ‘Interns’.



A list of domains/areas for which internship is invited is enclosed as Annexure ‘A’.

Objectives of the Scheme:

The Scheme has the following stated objectives:

  1. To allow young academic talent to be associated with the PMU-CARES work for mutual benefit.
  2. The ‘Interns’ shall have an opportunity to know about the PMU-CARES functioning and develop various hardware/software tools electronic kits, learning material and contribute to the Skill-based competence development on Computational Thinking/ Design Thinking/STREAM implementation and roll-out by implementing the various tools/techniques using Research knowledge, empirical study reports for school education and review various Government policy papers, etc. in similar domain.

Internship Details:

Specified Duration of Internship:

(a) For Undergraduate/ Postgraduate:

6 weeks (min.) to 6 months (max.)

(b) For Reasearch Scholars:

12 months

Instructions for Applicants:

  1. Interested applicants must apply online through the PMU-CARES website, using the following link:
    https://forms.gle/WR2ZkrHduaakRZkh7
  2. Candidates can download the Application Form using the following link:
    Application-Form-GMMPY
  3. The scanned copy of duly signed Application Form should be emailed at:
    cares-admin@goa.gov.in
  4. Clearly indicate the area of interest in your application.
  5. Ensure that all eligibility criteria are met before applying.
  6. Selected candidates will be required to produce original documents and No Objection Certificate (NOC) at the time of joining.

Annexure-A:

List of Domains/ Areas for Internship

The Committee consists of following members:

  1. Data Management and Analysis.
  2. Economics of School Education.
  3. Human Resources Development for School Education.
  4. Governance of School Education.
  5. Industry support for School Education.
  6. Last Mile Infrastructure connectivity for Schools.
  7. Mass Communications and Social Media impact on School Education.
  8. Programme Monitoring and Evaluation.
  9. Public Finances/Budget for School Education.
  10. Public Private Partnership for School Education.
  11. Skill Development & Employment
  12. Longitudinal research on reach & impact of skill-based education.
  13. Computational thinking/Design thinking implementation and roll out.
  14. Cross-domain study for Project based learning
  15. Divyang support – Towards Inclusive Education with focus on skill-based Education
  16. Migrant kids adopting to normal schooling
  17. State assessment survey on Science & Technology using Project Based Learning

Note: Domains/ Areas for Internship may be added/ deleted as per the needs and at the discretion of the Project Director-Project Management Unit-Coding and Robitics Education in Schools (CARES) Scheme.
