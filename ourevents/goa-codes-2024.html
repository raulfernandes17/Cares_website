GOA CODES 2024

Goa Codes 2024 will be held in 3 phases

  • Scheduled on : Tentatively on or before 16th March 2024.
  • Eligibility : Students enrolled for Class VI Elective Curriculum for A.Y. 2023-24
  • The Theme for the Competition :
    • Coding Adventures: Craft an interactive story using the available extensions in block coding. Imagine your virtual journey, a mystery to solve, or an imaginative world of your own.


  • Scheduled on : 24th & 25th June 2024
  • Eligibility : Winners of Goa Codes 2024 – Phase I – Lead School Level.
  • The Theme for the Competition : Eco-Tech Vision: Showcase your vision for a sustainable future. Highlight the importance of environmental conservation.


    • Rules for the competition:

    • Students are requested to attend the Taluka Level Competition at the assigned Location and during the assigned Batch-Time.
    • Students are required to wear the school uniform and carry Identity Proof for the Competition.
    • Participants should reach the location assigned to him / her for the competition at least 15 min in advance.
    • Students have to participate individually and NOT as a team at Taluka Level competition.
    • For the Lead School Level competition, students would be required to use tools / technology learnt during Elective Curriculum A.Y.2024-25 as per the Competition Categories.
    • A student can submit only one project.
    • It is crucial for students to maintain discipline within the premises of the school assigned for the competition.
