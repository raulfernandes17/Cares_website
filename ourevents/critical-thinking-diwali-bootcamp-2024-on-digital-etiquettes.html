Diwali Bootcamp 2024 – on ‘Digital Etiquettes’

  • Two day ‘Digital Etiquettes’ Boot Camp will be conducted on 14th and 15th November 2024.
  • Registration begins from 10.00 AM on 4th November till 3.00 PM on 10th November 2024.
  • Participants: Students of class V, VI, VII, VIII and their 01 accompanying Parent of AY 2024-25, of all schools across the state of Goa, including schools other than Goa State Board.
  • Mode of Conduct: Offline (In Person mode)
  • Maximum two batches per selected schools.
  • Batch Schedule : Batch I – 9.00 am to 11.00 am, Batch II – 11.30 am to 1.30 pm
  • Registration confirmation is on a First Come First Serve basis.
  • Students are free to select their preferred Bootcamp Location and Batch within their Taluka as provided in the Registration Form.
  • Once Bootcamp Location and Batch selection is submitted it cannot be changed after the Registration closes.
  • Kindly note : There is no Registration Fee for the Students attending the Boot camp.
  • Registration does not indicate confirmation of the student for the Boot Camp.
  • Boot Camp. The CONFIRMED LIST of students will be put up on 11th November 2024 on the CARES Website https://cares.goa.gov.in/
