Goa Codes 2025

Goa Codes 2025 is being held to celebrate and showcase the tech-savvy minds of Goan school students and witness the magic they create using Block Based Coding and Robotics Hardware Kit using GOVIN IDE.
The objective -> is to create awareness of the U.N Sustainable Development Goals px
(UNSDG) through concepts learnt during the revised ICT elective curriculum for AY 2024-25.
The competition will be held in the following Competition Categories
(a) Class VI Elective Category

Projects focused on Design Thinking and Coding (including Block-based projects).

ELIGIBILITY Students enrolled for Class VI Elective Curriculum for A.Y. 2024-25


(b) Class VII Elective Category

Projects centered around Robotics Hardware Kit using GOVIN IDE.

ELIGIBILITY Students enrolled for Class VII Elective Curriculum for A.Y. 2024-25

  • A student can submit only one project.
  • It is crucial for students to maintain discipline within the premises of the school assigned for the competition.
